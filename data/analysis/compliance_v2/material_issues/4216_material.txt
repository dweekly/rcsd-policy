MATERIAL COMPLIANCE ISSUES
BP 4216: Probationary/Permanent Status
Last Reviewed: 12/07/2022
================================================================================

Missing Notification Procedures for Permanent Status (Confidence: 85%)
Missing from: BOTH
The policy does not specify how employees will be notified when they achieve permanent status, nor does it establish procedures for tracking and documenting the transition from probationary to permanent status.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code 45113: Districts must have clear procedures for determining and documenting when employees achieve permanent status after completing probationary requirements.

Required Language:
Procedures must be established for: tracking probationary period completion, notifying employees of permanent status achievement, updating personnel records, and documenting the effective date of permanent status.

Recommended Placement: AR
