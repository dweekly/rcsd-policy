MATERIAL COMPLIANCE ISSUES
BP 3471: Parcel Taxes
Last Reviewed: 06/23/2021
================================================================================

Missing Specific Public Hearing Notice Requirements (Confidence: 90%)
Missing from: BOTH
The policy mentions "noticed public hearing" but does not specify the notice requirements mandated by law, including timing, content, and publication requirements for parcel tax hearings.

Legal Basis:
- Government Code 50077: Requires specific notice procedures for public hearings on parcel tax proposals, including timing and publication requirements

Required Language:
Public hearing notice shall be published at least 10 days prior to the hearing in a newspaper of general circulation within the district, and shall include the time, date, and location of the hearing, the purpose of the proposed tax, and the proposed tax rate.

Recommended Placement: AR
