Last Reviewed: 06/23/2010
================================================================================

Missing April Constitution Exercise Requirement (Confidence: 85%)
Missing from: BOTH
The policy only addresses September 17 Constitution Day but fails to include the mandatory Constitution exercises required before the last Friday in April each year for grades 4-12.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code Section 37221: Each school district and county office of education shall, on or before the last Friday in April of each year, conduct oral or written exercises for pupils in grades 4 to 12, inclusive, pertaining to the Constitution of the United States.

Required Language:
Add provision: "On or before the last Friday in April of each year, the district shall conduct oral or written exercises for pupils in grades 4 to 8, inclusive, pertaining to the Constitution of the United States."

Recommended Placement: BP
