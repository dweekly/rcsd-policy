MATERIAL COMPLIANCE ISSUES
BP 4300: Administrative And Supervisory Personnel
Last Reviewed: 11/04/2009
================================================================================

Missing Performance Evaluation Requirements (Confidence: 80%)
Missing from: BOTH
The policy states that management personnel should demonstrate initiative and good judgment, but lacks specific evaluation criteria, processes, or timelines required by California law for administrative personnel evaluation.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code Section 35031: It shall be the duty of the superintendent of schools of the county to evaluate the performance of each superintendent of schools of school districts under his or her jurisdiction.

Required Language:
Policy should establish evaluation requirements for administrative personnel. Administrative Regulation should detail evaluation criteria, timeline (typically annual), evaluation instruments, improvement plans for unsatisfactory performance, and documentation requirements.

Recommended Placement: AR
