MATERIAL COMPLIANCE ISSUES
BP 5123: Promotion/Acceleration/Retention
Last Reviewed: 01/13/2010
================================================================================

Missing Supplemental Instruction Program Requirements (Confidence: 85%)
Missing from: BOTH
Education Code 37252.2 requires specific components for supplemental instruction programs offered to retained students in grades 2-8, including direct instruction, systematic instruction, intensive instruction, and daily assessments. While the BP mentions offering supplemental instruction "in accordance with Education Code 37252.2," neither document specifies the required program components.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code 37252.2(a): The program of supplemental instruction shall include direct instruction, systematic instruction, and intensive instruction and shall provide daily assessments of pupil progress.

Required Language:
Supplemental instruction programs for retained students in grades 2-8 shall include: (1) Direct instruction, (2) Systematic instruction, (3) Intensive instruction, and (4) Daily assessments of pupil progress.

Recommended Placement: AR

----------------------------------------

Missing Parent Notification Requirements for Supplemental Instruction (Confidence: 90%)
Missing from: BOTH
Education Code 37252.2 requires districts to notify parents/guardians about supplemental instruction programs, including information about the program and the option to decline participation. This notification requirement is completely absent from both documents.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code 37252.2(b): The school district shall notify the parent or guardian of a pupil who is recommended for or who is retained in the same grade level for a second or subsequent time that supplemental instruction will be made available to the pupil. The parent or guardian may decline to have the pupil participate in the supplemental instruction.

Required Language:
The district shall notify parents/guardians of students recommended for or retained in grades 2-8 that supplemental instruction will be made available. Parents/guardians may decline to have their child participate in supplemental instruction.

Recommended Placement: AR
