MATERIAL COMPLIANCE ISSUES
BP 1321: Solicitation Of Funds From And By Students
Last Reviewed: 11/04/2009
================================================================================

Missing Required Application and Documentation Procedures (Confidence: 90%)
Missing from: BOTH
Education Code 51520 requires specific application procedures for charitable organizations seeking to solicit students, but neither the BP nor AR contains these procedural requirements.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code 51520(b): Any organization desiring to make solicitations shall make application to the governing board or its designee and shall furnish evidence of the charitable nature and financial responsibility of the organization.

Required Language:
Procedures requiring charitable organizations to: (1) submit formal applications to the Superintendent or designee, (2) provide evidence of charitable nature and financial responsibility, (3) submit required documentation before approval.

Recommended Placement: AR

----------------------------------------

Missing Supervision Requirements During Solicitations (Confidence: 85%)
Missing from: BOTH
Education Code requires that solicitations be conducted under school supervision, but neither document specifies supervision procedures or requirements.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code 51520(c): All solicitations shall be made under the supervision of the school authorities.

Required Language:
Procedures requiring that all charitable solicitations be conducted under direct supervision of designated school personnel, including specification of supervision responsibilities and oversight requirements.

Recommended Placement: AR
