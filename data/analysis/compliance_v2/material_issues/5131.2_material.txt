MATERIAL COMPLIANCE ISSUES
BP 5131.2: Bullying
Last Reviewed: 11/06/2019
================================================================================

Missing Annual Staff Notification Requirement (Confidence: 90%)
Missing from: BOTH
Education Code 234.1 requires annual notification to employees about their duty to intervene when witnessing bullying. While the AR mentions this requirement in the prevention measures section, there is no clear statement that this notification will occur annually as mandated.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code 234.1: Each school district, county office of education, and charter school shall annually make available the online training module developed by the department pursuant to Section 32283.5 to certificated schoolsite employees and all other schoolsite employees who have regular interaction with children.

Required Language:
The district shall annually notify all employees of their duty to immediately intervene when it is safe to do so if they witness an act of bullying, and shall annually make available online training modules on bullying dynamics to all certificated staff and other employees who have regular interaction with students.

Recommended Placement: AR
