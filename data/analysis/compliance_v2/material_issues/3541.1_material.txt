MATERIAL COMPLIANCE ISSUES
AR 3541.1: Transportation For School-Related Trips
Last Reviewed: 11/04/2009
================================================================================

Missing Driver Background Check Requirements (Confidence: 90%)
Missing from: BOTH
Neither the BP nor AR addresses background check requirements for private vehicle drivers transporting students. California law requires background checks for individuals who have contact with students in school-sponsored activities.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code Section 45125.1: Requires criminal background checks for individuals who have contact with pupils in a school-sponsored activity away from school grounds.

Required Language:
All drivers of private vehicles transporting students must complete criminal background checks, including DOJ and FBI clearances, prior to approval for student transportation.

Recommended Placement: AR

----------------------------------------

Missing Emergency Procedures and Communication Plan (Confidence: 85%)
Missing from: BOTH
The regulation mentions that drivers shall receive "safety and emergency instructions" but does not specify what these must include or establish emergency communication protocols required for student safety during transportation.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code Section 32221: School districts must establish procedures for emergency situations involving students.

Required Language:
Emergency procedures must include: communication protocols with school administration, procedures for accidents or breakdowns, emergency contact information for all passengers, first aid requirements, and procedures for severe weather or other emergencies.

Recommended Placement: AR

----------------------------------------

Missing Commercial Transportation Standards (Confidence: 80%)
Missing from: BOTH
The regulation does not address requirements when using commercial transportation services (buses, charter services) including driver qualifications, vehicle safety standards, and insurance requirements for contracted services.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code Section 39831: Establishes requirements for pupil transportation safety and vehicle standards.

Required Language:
When using commercial transportation services, the district shall verify that drivers meet all state certification requirements, vehicles meet safety standards, and carriers maintain appropriate insurance coverage and safety records.

Recommended Placement: AR
