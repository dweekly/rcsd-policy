MATERIAL COMPLIANCE ISSUES
AR 4112.5: Criminal Record Check
Last Reviewed: 11/04/2009
================================================================================

Missing Coverage for Classified Employees (Confidence: 90%)
Missing from: BOTH
The regulation only addresses certificated employees for conviction-related termination procedures, but Education Code requires criminal background checks for all employees. The regulation lacks procedures for handling classified employees with disqualifying convictions.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code 45125.1: Prohibits employment of classified employees convicted of violent or serious felonies, with similar procedures as certificated staff

Required Language:
Procedures for classified employees parallel to those for certificated employees, including immediate placement on unpaid leave upon conviction notification and termination procedures unless employee has certificate of rehabilitation and pardon

Recommended Placement: AR

----------------------------------------

Missing Volunteer Background Check Requirements (Confidence: 85%)
Missing from: BOTH
The regulation does not address criminal background check requirements for volunteers who have frequent or prolonged contact with students, which is required by California law.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code 45125.1: Requires background checks for volunteers who will have frequent or prolonged contact with students

Required Language:
Procedures for conducting criminal background checks on volunteers who will have frequent or prolonged contact with students, including fingerprinting requirements and disqualification criteria

Recommended Placement: AR

----------------------------------------

Missing DOJ Clearance Timeline Requirements (Confidence: 80%)
Missing from: BOTH
The regulation does not specify the required timeline for receiving DOJ clearance before employment begins, which is mandated by state law.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code 44830.1, 45125.1: Requires that no person may be employed until DOJ clearance is received, with limited exceptions for emergency situations

Required Language:
No person shall be employed until DOJ clearance is received. Emergency employment procedures may be used only when specific conditions are met and for limited time periods as specified in law

Recommended Placement: AR
