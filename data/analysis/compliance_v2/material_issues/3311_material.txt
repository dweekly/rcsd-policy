Missing Best Value Contracting Procedures (Confidence: 85%)
Missing from: BOTH
California law allows school districts to use best value contracting for certain projects, but neither document addresses this procurement method, limiting the district's procurement options.

Legal Basis:
- Public Contract Code Section 20111.6: Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the governing board of a school district may use a best value procurement process to award contracts for the procurement of goods and services.

Required Language:
Authorization and procedures for best value procurement including evaluation criteria, scoring methodology, and award procedures when price is not the sole determining factor.
