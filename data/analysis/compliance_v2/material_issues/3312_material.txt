Incomplete Public Hearing Requirements for Non-Nutritious Food/Beverage Contracts (Confidence: 90%)
Missing from: BOTH
The policy mentions public hearing requirements for non-nutritious food and beverage contracts but fails to include all required elements. The policy discusses hearing content but doesn't clearly establish the requirement for a noticed public hearing before entering such contracts.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code 35182.5(a): Before a school district or a school enters into or renews a contract that grants exclusive or nonexclusive advertising or sale of beverages or food, the governing board of the school district shall hold a public hearing.

Required Language:
Before the district or a district school enters into or renews a contract for non-nutritious food or beverages, the Board shall hold a noticed public hearing. The hearing shall include discussion of nutritional value, availability of fresh produce, fat/sugar/additive content, and barriers to meal program participation.

Recommended Placement: BP
