MATERIAL COMPLIANCE ISSUES
BP 4115: Evaluation/Supervision
Last Reviewed: 11/04/2009
================================================================================

Missing Peer Assistance and Review Program Requirements (Confidence: 95%)
Missing from: BOTH
The regulation mentions that employees with unsatisfactory ratings must participate in the peer assistance and review program, but fails to include the mandatory establishment and operational requirements for such programs. Districts must either establish their own PAR program or participate in a joint program, with specific governance, funding, and procedural requirements.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code 44500-44508: Every school district shall establish a peer assistance and review program for certificated employees or participate in a peer assistance and review program operated jointly by multiple employers... The program shall include specific procedures for the selection of consulting teachers, training requirements, and evaluation processes.

Required Language:
The district shall establish and maintain a peer assistance and review program in accordance with Education Code 44500-44508, including procedures for selecting consulting teachers, training requirements, program governance through a joint panel of teachers and administrators, and funding mechanisms. The program shall provide assistance to participating teachers and conduct evaluations as required by law.
