Missing Emergency Closure and Makeup Day Procedures (Confidence: 85%)
Missing from: BOTH
Neither the policy nor regulation addresses procedures for emergency school closures or requirements for makeup days to ensure minimum instructional time is maintained, which is required by state law.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code Section 37223: If any school is closed for any cause, the governing board of the school district may require the school to be taught for the number of days lost by the closing.

Required Language:
The district shall establish procedures for emergency school closures and shall determine when makeup days are required to ensure compliance with minimum instructional day requirements.

Recommended Placement: AR
