MATERIAL COMPLIANCE ISSUES
BP 6300: Preschool/Early Childhood Education
Last Reviewed: 01/23/2013
================================================================================

Missing Specific Staff Qualification Requirements (Confidence: 90%)
Missing from: BOTH
Education Code Section 8244 requires specific qualification standards for preschool staff that go beyond the general reference to credentials in the policy. Detailed requirements for teachers, aides, and administrators must be specified.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code Section 8244: Teachers in state preschool programs shall hold a valid teaching credential with specialization in early childhood education or child development, and meet additional experience requirements as specified.

Required Language:
Preschool teachers must hold: (1) A valid California teaching credential with early childhood education or child development specialization, or (2) A child development teacher permit, plus specified units in child development and supervised experience with preschool-age children. Aides must meet minimum qualification requirements including child development coursework.

Recommended Placement: AR

----------------------------------------

Missing Health and Safety Requirements (Confidence: 85%)
Missing from: BOTH
Title 5 CCR Section 18275 mandates specific health and safety standards for preschool programs that are not adequately addressed in the current policy.

Legal Basis:
- Title 5 CCR Section 18275: State preschool programs shall comply with health and safety requirements including immunization verification, health screening, emergency procedures, and facility safety standards.

Required Language:
Preschool programs must establish procedures for: verification of required immunizations, health and developmental screenings, emergency evacuation procedures, accident reporting, medication administration, and compliance with facility licensing requirements.

Recommended Placement: AR
