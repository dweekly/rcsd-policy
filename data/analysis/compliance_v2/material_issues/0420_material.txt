BP 0420: School Plans/Site Councils
Last Reviewed: 05/23/2018
================================================================================

Missing English Learner Parent Advisory Requirements (Confidence: 90%)
Missing from: BOTH
For schools with significant English learner populations, Education Code requires specific provisions for English learner parent participation in the development of the SPSA, which is not addressed in either document.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code 64001(j): If the school has a significant number of pupils who are English learners, the plan shall be developed, reviewed, and approved with the advice of parents or guardians of English learners and with the advice of teachers with appropriate training to serve English learners.

Required Language:
If the school has a significant number of pupils who are English learners, the single plan for student achievement shall be developed, reviewed, and approved with the advice of parents or guardians of English learners and with the advice of teachers with appropriate training to serve English learners.

Recommended Placement: AR
