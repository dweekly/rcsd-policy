MATERIAL COMPLIANCE ISSUES
BP 6163.1: Library Media Centers
Last Reviewed: 06/23/2010
================================================================================

Missing Library Material Complaint Procedures (Confidence: 85%)
Missing from: BOTH
The policy references that complaints regarding library materials shall use "the district's procedures for complaints regarding instructional materials" but does not specify what those procedures are or ensure they address library-specific considerations.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code 35186: Each school district shall establish procedures for receiving and investigating complaints and for remedying any valid complaints.

Required Language:
Specific procedures must be established for handling complaints about library materials including: (1) formal complaint form and submission process, (2) review committee composition including library professionals, (3) evaluation criteria and timeline for review, (4) appeal process, and (5) interim procedures for challenged materials during review.

Recommended Placement: AR
