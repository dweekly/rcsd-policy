MATERIAL COMPLIANCE ISSUES
BP 5141.52: Suicide Prevention
Last Reviewed: 03/11/2020
================================================================================

Missing Specific Training Duration Requirement (Confidence: 90%)
Missing from: BOTH
Education Code 215 requires that suicide prevention training be at least one hour in duration. Neither the BP nor AR specifies the minimum duration requirement for the training.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code Section 215(c)(2): The training required by paragraph (1) shall be at least one hour and shall include training on how to identify the warning signs of suicidal behavior in adolescents and teens and how to respond appropriately to students who may be contemplating suicide.

Required Language:
The suicide prevention training shall be at least one hour in duration.

Recommended Placement: AR
