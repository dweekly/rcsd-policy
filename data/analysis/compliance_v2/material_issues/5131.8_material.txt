MATERIAL COMPLIANCE ISSUES
BP 5131.8: Mobile Communication Devices
Last Reviewed: 01/13/2021
================================================================================

Missing Parent Notification Requirements (Confidence: 85%)
Missing from: BOTH
When mobile devices are confiscated from students, parents have a right to be notified. The policy does not address when and how parents will be informed of confiscation, which could violate due process requirements and create liability issues.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code Section 48900.5: Suspension, including supervised suspension, or expulsion may be imposed only when other means of correction fail to bring about proper conduct.

Required Language:
Parents/guardians must be notified within [specific timeframe] when a student's mobile communication device is confiscated. Notification shall include the reason for confiscation, duration of confiscation, and procedures for return.

Recommended Placement: AR
