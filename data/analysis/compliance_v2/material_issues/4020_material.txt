MATERIAL COMPLIANCE ISSUES
BP 4020: Drug And Alcohol-Free Workplace
Last Reviewed: 11/04/2009
================================================================================

Missing Good Faith Effort Requirement for Rehabilitation (Confidence: 90%)
Missing from: BOTH
Federal law requires that employers make a good faith effort to continue to maintain a drug-free workplace, which includes specific requirements for employee rehabilitation efforts that are not detailed in the current policy.

Legal Basis:
- 41 USC 8104: The contractor or grantee shall make a good faith effort to continue to maintain a drug-free workplace through implementation of this section.

Required Language:
The district shall make a good faith effort to maintain a drug-free workplace through implementation of this policy, including taking appropriate personnel action against employees who violate the policy and making good faith efforts to ensure employees participate in rehabilitation programs when required.

Recommended Placement: BP
