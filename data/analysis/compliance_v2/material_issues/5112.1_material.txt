MATERIAL COMPLIANCE ISSUES
BP 5112.1: Exemptions From Attendance
Last Reviewed: 01/13/2010
================================================================================

Missing Exemption Process and Criteria (Confidence: 85%)
Missing from: BOTH
Neither the policy nor regulation specifies the process for requesting exemptions, required documentation, approval criteria, or appeal procedures. This creates ambiguity in implementation and potential due process concerns.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code Section 48221: Pupils subject to compulsory full-time education may be exempted by the governing board of the school district under such rules and regulations as may be prescribed by the governing board and approved by the county superintendent of schools.

Required Language:
The Superintendent or designee shall establish procedures for reviewing and approving exemption requests, including required documentation, timelines for decisions, and appeal processes. All exemption decisions must be approved by the county superintendent of schools as required by law.

Recommended Placement: AR
