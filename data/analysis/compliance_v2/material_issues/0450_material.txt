MATERIAL COMPLIANCE ISSUES
BP 0450: Comprehensive Safety Plan
Last Reviewed: 11/04/2009
================================================================================

Missing Requirement for Safety Plan Copies to Law Enforcement (Confidence: 95%)
Missing from: BOTH
California law requires that copies of the comprehensive school safety plan be provided to law enforcement agencies that are the designated first responders to the school. This requirement is completely absent from both the policy and regulation.

Legal Basis:
- Education Code Section 32282(a)(2): The comprehensive school safety plan shall be provided to the law enforcement agency or agencies that are the designated first responders to the school for emergency situations.

Required Language:
The Superintendent or designee shall provide copies of each school's comprehensive safety plan to the law enforcement agency or agencies that are the designated first responders to that school for emergency situations.

Recommended Placement: AR
