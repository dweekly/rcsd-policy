RCSD Regulation 5111.16
================================================================================
Title: Residency For Homeless Children
Status: ADOPTED
Original Adopted Date: 01/13/2010
Last Reviewed Date: 01/13/2010
Source: RCSD Policies 5000.pdf (Pages 45)
================================================================================

Homeless students living in the district shall be admitted to district schools upon presentation of any of the
following:
1. Hotel or motel receipts
2. A letter from a social service agency or homeless shelter verifying that the student lives within the district
3. An affidavit from the parent/guardian stating that the family lives within the district
A reasonable effort shall be made to secure an address, phone number and medical release from the parent/guardian
when a student is placed in a classroom.
These references are not intended to be part of the policy itself, nor do they indicate the basis or authority
for the Governing Board to enact this policy. Instead, they are provided as additional resources for those interested in the subject matter
of the policy.
